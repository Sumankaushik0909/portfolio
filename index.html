Professional Experience

Mrs. Suman Kaushik
Educational Leadership | CBSE Initiatives | Student Wellbeing

With over a decade of experience in academic leadership, Suman Kaushik has transformed classrooms into learner-centric spaces and guided teachers toward excellence. She blends academic rigor with life-skills education, mentoring both staff and students to thrive in CBSE-aligned environments. Equipped with an MA in Economics and an M.Ed., along with advanced training in school administration, adolescent development, and counseling, she brings a holistic approach to education.

Vision and Leadership

Guided by CBSE’s vision and NEP 2020 priorities, she focuses on nurturing responsible, future-ready citizens. Her initiatives balance academic performance with cultural growth, environmental awareness, and student wellbeing. Highlights include directing the Karate Championship in Sonipat to promote youth fitness under national programs, while also empowering teachers through professional development workshops that encourage innovation and collaboration. Beyond awards, her leadership has been featured in national media for innovation in academics, extracurricular excellence, and student counseling.

Achievements

Her work has been honored and widely recognized, reflecting both institutional and system-level contributions:

  • Outstanding School Leader Award – National Eduleaders Summit 2025
  • Vivekananda Excellence Award
  • Avantika Shikshak Samman
  • Paryavaran Dronacharya Award
  • Recognition from Bharat Vikas Parishad for National Programs
  • Best Cultural Coordinator Award

Empowering the Future

A strong advocate for gender equality, she has championed the empowerment of girls through education, ensuring access to resources and opportunities that help them break barriers and pursue their dreams. Her focus extends beyond academic achievement, nurturing resilience, compassion, and responsibility in every learner. With her expertise in counseling and capacity building, she is now preparing to expand her impact as an educational psychologist and CBSE advisor.

"Education must go beyond textbooks; my mission is to provide the guidance, wellbeing, and opportunities every learner needs to discover their purpose and shine in their own extraordinary way.”
– Mrs. Suman Kaushik

Achievements in Spotlight

Honored with the Outstanding School Leader Award at the National Eduleaders Summit 2025, Suman Kaushik has been recognized nationally for her vision, disciplined execution, and measurable impact on student outcomes. Her work reflects a balance of academics, sports, and values, transforming institutions into spaces of holistic learning. She has promoted youth engagement through the Khel Maha Kumbh District Karate Championship in Sonipat, strengthened teacher capacity with CBSE training on adolescent education in Panchkula, contributed to institutional learning during a CBSE exposure visit at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, and applied progressive practices from Amity University, Mohali workshops on positive learning. Leading newspapers have highlighted these efforts, noting her role in advancing academic excellence, expanding opportunities in sports, and building character in students. Through consistent ground-level work and effective mentoring, she continues to position herself as an educational leader dedicated to nurturing confident, disciplined, and socially responsible citizens.
